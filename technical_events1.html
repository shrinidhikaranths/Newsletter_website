TECHNICAL EVENTS

  • Hackloop

    Hackloop is a series of internal hackathons organized by the Finite Loop Club (FLC) at NMAM Institute of Technology (NMAMIT), Nitte, for first and second-year students to gain hands-on experience in coding, collaboration, and project-based learning. For instance, Hackloop S03 in 2024 was an internal event focused on development, collaboration, and problem-solving, running from October to November, and included a prize pool. The event helps students work with real-world problems, often using technologies and methodologies like Git and agile development.

    About Hackloop


    Organizer: The Finite Loop Club (FLC) at NMAMIT, Nitte.
    Target Audience: Primarily first and second-year students, specifically members of the Finite Loop Club.
    Event Format: An internal hackathon focused on building and coding, often with a specific theme or problem statement provided.
    Purpose: To provide students with practical, project-based learning experience in a collaborative environment, helping them develop skills in development, problem-solving, and teamwork.

    Key Features:


    Mentorship: Offers mentorship to participants.
    Prizes and Perks: Includes prizes, merchandise, and other benefits for participants.
    Skill Development: Focuses on hands-on experience with tools and methodologies such as Git and agile development.



  • AINNOVATION II 2025

    The "AINNOVATION II 2025:" event at NMAMIT, Nitte was a 24-hour AI hackathon held on September 23-24, 2025, organized with Kyndryl and Microsoft to create technology-driven solutions for societal needs. It included a 30+ team hackathon with participants from various colleges in Karnataka and featured a "Curtain Raiser" event on August 14, 2025, which was inaugurated by Kyndryl and Microsoft officials and involved 90 participants from 15 colleges.
    AINNOVATION II 2025:
    Event Type: 24-hour AI Hackathon.
    Date: September 23-24, 2025.
    Organizers: NMAM Institute of Technology, Nitte in collaboration with Kyndryl and Microsoft.
    Purpose: To develop technology-driven solutions for societal needs.
    Participants: Over 30 teams from colleges across Karnataka.


  • HACKFEST

    Hackfest is an annual national-level tech fest held at NMAM Institute of Technology (NMAMIT) in Nitte, organized by the Finite Loop Club. The event features a 36-hour hackathon, tech conferences with industry speakers, and networking opportunities, bringing together teams from engineering colleges across India. It serves as a platform for participants to innovate and collaborate on solutions to real-world challenges across different themes, with prizes awarded to the winners.
    Core components 36-hour hackathon: The main competition where teams work intensely to develop innovative solutions within a set time limit and theme.Tech conferences: Talks and sessions are held with high-profile speakers and representatives from sponsoring companies, covering current topics in technology.Networking: A key aspect of the event, providing opportunities for participants to connect with the team, mentors, and industry experts.
    Other activities: In addition to the hackathon and conferences, the event includes other activities to foster a lively tech fest atmosphere. Organization and participation
    Organizer: Finite Loop Club at NMAM Institute of Technology.Participants: Teams from top engineering colleges across India. Structure: A three-day event that includes a 50-hour on-site duration with a 36-hour hackathon at its core. Key features from past events
    Themes: Events have featured themes such as "Sustainable Business," "Preventing Digital Fraud," and "Ethics in AI Models for Business".
    Sponsors: Notable sponsors have included SAP, Palo Alto Networks and Rakuten.
    Prizes: Significant prize money has been awarded to winners, with one event offering a total of rupees 2,50,000.
